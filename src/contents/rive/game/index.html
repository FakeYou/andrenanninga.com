RIVE

RIVE is a game about evolution! By splitting you create mutations of yourself which have slighty different properties. Each offspring has the potienal to become a great lineage! But each offspring also need control and since multitasking is hard you just control all at once! Which offspring will you favor and lead to a great destiny, which will you condemn to a quick but brutal death!

You can control your offspring with W A S D. By pressing SPACE you can split to double your offspring. Press R for restart and Esc for a quick break!

This game was created for Ludum Dare 24 with the theme Evolution. The entry can be found here. It was created in 48 hours by Andre "FakeYou" Nanninga.
